Final seed of the exp4 multi-seed confirmation at 1024×1024, batch 8. Active mAP50 = 0.699 — the highest of the three mosaic@1024 seeds (range 0.683–0.699).

Highest Active mAP50 of the final detector configuration: mosaic augmentation at 512×512, batch 16, positives-only. Active mAP50 = 0.741 — the best valid run (mosaic_mixup's occasional higher scores were confirmed as single-seed noise in multi-seed testing).

Augmentation fixes the floor's overfitting: val-mAP peak shifts from epoch ~12 to ~150, and medium-lesion recall doubles from 38.9% to 51.4%. Catches 118/121 TB cases at conf=0.25 — a 97.5% detection rate — with only 5.8% healthy false alarm rate.

Multi-seed validation (exp4) confirmed mosaic beats geometry-only augmentation across seeds at 1024px with non-overlapping confidence intervals. The final detector configuration decided: mosaic @ 512, batch 16. Carried into exp5 (k-fold CV).
